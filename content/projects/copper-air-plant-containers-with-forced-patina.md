These little copper cups were made in the 1970s and in awesome condition when we found them. They make great little planters for small plants. I did a non-shipwreck forced patina on them to bring out some cool patterns and a range of dark colors. (Some form of teal/cobalt also comes through even without the sea salt, which I do not hate.) I think they look really cool. I put some air plants in them and gave them away as gifts.
